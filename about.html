About Me

Interests

Movies

As a passionate movie buff, nothing beats the magic of the theater experience. Whether it's the latest animated flick, an action-packed adventure, or a laugh-out-loud comedy, I'm always up for it. And as a die-hard Star Wars fan, I'll never tire of a galaxy far, far away. I also have a soft spot for movies that are so bad, they're good.

Trivia

I'm a lifelong learner and love to share interesting facts with others. I strive to be well-versed in many areas and enjoy connecting with people through knowledge. Some even call me a modern-day polymath.

Hobbies

Hiking

To balance out my screen time, I love to explore the great outdoors. Going on hikes with friends is the perfect way to disconnect and enjoy the beauty of nature.

Making Things

I'm a hands-on person who loves to building things. While I enjoy working in cyberspace, there's nothing like seeing a real world project come together before my eyes. Whether it's hanging drywall or building a bookshelf, I relish the opportunity to work with tools and hone my skills.

Reading

I'm an avid reader with interests spanning from astronomy to zoology and everything in between. Though I mostly read non-fiction, I also enjoy a good scifi fantasy novel.
